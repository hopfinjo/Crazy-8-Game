Methods

takeATurn()

Play for the computer, updating the computer's hand as well as the deck and pile as appropriate. In this version, the computer always plays the first card in its hand that is playable. If it plays an 8, the suit "announced" is the suit on the card. This method does not update the display; that is handled by the Presenter.
