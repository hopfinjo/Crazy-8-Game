Class: HumanPlayer

HumanPlayer(deck, pile, view)

The human player's hand and handlers for responding to his or her actions, including picking a card from the deck, selecting a card to play from the hand, and selecting a suit after an 8 has been played. These methods are responsible for all updates of the view (UI) in response to human player processing.
